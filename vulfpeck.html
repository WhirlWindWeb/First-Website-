Vulfpeck

Vulfpeck is an American funk group founded in 2011.
The band has released four EPs, six albums, several compilations,
and a silent album on Spotify titled Sleepify, royalties from which
funded Vulfpeck's admission-free tour in 2014.


The band releases music independently through Vulf Records,
which also publishes a host of other side projects by band members.
